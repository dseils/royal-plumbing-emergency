## Key Design Elements

- **Colors:** Red (urgency/emergency), White (clean/trust), Dark Gray (professional)
- **Typography:** Bold headlines, clear hierarchy
- **Mobile-First:** Giant tap-to-call buttons, minimal scrolling
- **Conversion Focused:** Phone number prominent, no distracting forms
- **Trust Signals:** Licensed, insured, local, 5-star reviews

## Conversion Optimization Features

✅ Phone number in header (immediate action)  
✅ Pattern interrupt headline ("Burst Pipe at 2 AM?")  
✅ Cost comparison (creates urgency)  
✅ 3 clear differentiators  
✅ Service grid (shows capability)  
✅ Social proof (builds trust)  
✅ Final CTA (closes the deal)  
✅ Pulsing call button (draws attention)
